⬡ PRO STARTUP GUIDE
01 Stem Separation AI Brain

Before 5.1 upmixing, the AI splits your stereo track into isolated instrument layers called stems.

htdemucs_6s Recommended 6 stems · Vocals / Drums / Bass / Guitar / Piano / Other
htdemucs_ft Fast Quicker render, slightly lower quality
spleeter Legacy 4–5 stems, compatible with older hardware
02 Studio Tuning Analog Color

Adds analog flavor — simulates real hardware circuitry to warm up digital audio.

SSL 4000 G Punchy, forward sound · Best for Rock / Pop
Neve 1073 Warm vintage harmonics · Best for Vocals / Acoustic
Tape Saturation Makes digital audio feel organic and "alive"
Bus Compression "Glues" the mix — makes it sound like one cohesive record
03 Loudness (LUFS) Normalization

LUFS = the platform's volume standard. Too loud → clipped. Too quiet → sounds weak in a playlist.

−14 LUFS Spotify · YouTube Industry streaming standard
−23 LUFS EBU R128 Broadcast / film / Netflix standard
FLAC Lossless Skips aggressive normalization — preserves full dynamic range
04 5.1 Spatial Routing Surround

6-channel surround layout. Each stem is routed to the appropriate speaker.

L / R Front left / right — main stereo mix
C Center channel — lead vocals, for clarity in home theater
Ls / Rs Rear speakers — atmospheres, reverb tails, backing layers
LFE (.1) Sub Subwoofer — bass frequencies below crossover Hz
