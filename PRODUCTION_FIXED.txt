## How to Use

1. Browser: http://127.0.0.1:8000

2. Drop audio file (WAV/FLAC/AIFF) - up to 500 MB

3. Configure pipeline settings (left panel)

4. Adjust studio tuning (⚙ button)

5. Click "▶ RUN FULL PIPELINE"

6. Wait for processing to complete

7. Click "⬇ EXPORT FILES" to download
